
# 📊 Sales Performance Analytics & KPI Monitoring

End-to-End Data Analytics Project | Sales Intelligence & Business Insights

---

## 📊 Project Overview

The **Sales Performance Analytics & KPI Monitoring System** is an end-to-end data analytics solution designed to track, analyze, and optimize business performance across sales, profit, and customer behavior.

This project transforms raw transactional data into **interactive dashboards and actionable insights**, enabling stakeholders to monitor KPIs, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

The solution integrates **Excel for data preprocessing, SQL for data transformation, and Power BI for visualization and reporting**.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Organizations often struggle with:

* Lack of visibility into **product-level performance**
* Inability to track **sales trends over time**
* Poor understanding of **profitability vs revenue**
* No structured way to compare **performance across time periods**
* Limited insights into **discount effectiveness and regional sales**
